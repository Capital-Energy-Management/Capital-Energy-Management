Energy Capital Management

ECM is a private investment fund focused
on Latin America's Oil & Gas sector.

We unlock long-term value by investing in companies with strong fundamentals, proven leadership, and scalable operations. At ECM, we combine financial discipline with regional insight to help our partners thrive in evolving energy markets.

Founded with a commitment to strategic value creation, ECM brings a deep understanding of the challenges and opportunities across Latin America's energy landscape.

We invest in companies at pivotal points in their growth—offering capital, advisory support, and access to our trusted network.

Our strategy focuses on high-performing businesses with the potential to lead in operational efficiency, sustainability, and innovation.

What we do

We deploy capital in two primary ways: direct equity investment and co-investment via sector-specialized funds. In both cases, we play an active role in guiding company growth—leveraging acquisitions, strategic alliances, and governance support to maximize impact.

How We Work

Our involvement doesn't end with a capital injection. We partner with management teams to build strong governance frameworks, streamline operations, and position companies for long-term resilience.

Our Focus

From upstream production to midstream services, ECM's focus spans the full energy value chain.
What sets us apart is our ability to combine rigorous investment analysis with real-time understanding of local markets and policy environments.
