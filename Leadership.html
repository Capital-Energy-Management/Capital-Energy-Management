Jorge Fergie

Managing Director

Mr. Fergie is the Managing Director of ECM and is based in São Paulo. He brings more than 30 years of experience advising, investing in, and operating across Latin America.

He spent nearly three decades at McKinsey & Company, where he played a central role in building the firm’s Latin American platform.

Prior to joining ECM, Mr. Fergie led KKR’s investment activities in Latin America, partnering closely with management teams to implement value-creation initiatives.

Mr. Fergie holds an M.Sc. in Industrial Engineering from Stanford University.
