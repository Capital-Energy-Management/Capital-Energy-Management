About Us

Founded With A Commitment To Strategic Value Creation, ECM Brings A Deep Understanding Of The Challenges And Opportunities Across Latin America's Energy Landscape. We Invest In Companies At Pivotal Points In Their Growth—Offering Capital, Advisory Support, And Access To Our Trusted Network.

Our Team Is Composed Of Seasoned Professionals With Backgrounds In Private Equity, Operations, And Public Policy. This Multidisciplinary Foundation Allows Us To Engage With Portfolio Companies In A Hands-On, Collaborative Manner. With Offices In São Paulo And Mexico City, We Maintain A Close Presence In The Region's Most Dynamic Energy Markets.

WHAT WE DO

ECM's Approach Is Built On A Long-Term Vision. We Understand That Energy Projects Require Time To Mature, And We Align Our Investments Accordingly. Our Strategy Focuses On High-Performing Businesses With The Potential To Lead In Operational Efficiency, Sustainability, And Innovation.

We Deploy Capital In Two Primary Ways: Direct Equity Investment And Co-Investment Via Sector-Specialized Funds. In Both Cases, We Play An Active Role In Guiding Company Growth—Leveraging Acquisitions, Strategic Alliances, And Governance Support To Maximize Impact.

Energy Capital Management (ECM) Is A Private Investment Fund Focused On Latin America's Oil & Gas Sector.
